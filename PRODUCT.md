# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Individual users, freelancers, and small business owners in Indonesia who need to track personal and business finances without tedious manual entry.

## Product Purpose
FinTrack is a localized financial tracking and analytics SaaS that consolidates cash flow by parsing bank statements (e.g., BNI, Bank Jago) and receipts via OCR. It eliminates manual entry overhead and provides aggregate net worth tracking, transaction history, and balance trend analytics.

## Brand Personality
- Trustworthy: Localized IDR formatting and precise data representations build absolute confidence.
- Professional: A clean, densified layout with a focused Indigo visual identity.
- Efficient: Snappy transitions, contextual split-screen reviewers, and immediate loading feedback.

## Anti-references
- "SaaS-cream" aesthetic: Warm neutral/beige AI-default color palettes.
- Over-roundedness: Card borders and button radii exceeding 12-16px.
- AI Slop patterns: Text overflow, generic cards with gradient kickers, and diagonal stripe backgrounds.
- Ambiguous Loading: Blocking spinner overlays without field-level skeleton animations.

## Design Principles
1. Precise Localization: Always use `id-ID` formatting with proper currency symbols and thousands separators.
2. Verified Data Integrity: Never hide the source material; always provide side-by-side comparison views for OCR corrections.
3. Structured Density: Prioritize readability of tabular finance data over empty space and soft shadows.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratio ≥ 4.5:1 for body and placeholder text to ensure legibility of numbers.
- Full support for reduced motion preferences during loading state transitions.
- Semantic HTML and keyboard-navigable interactive widgets (Comboboxes, Date Pickers).
